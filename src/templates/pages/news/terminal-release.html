BlockSettle launches the world’s first Bitcoin wallet with an integrated order management system

Malmo, Sweden, {{news['terminalrelease']['fullDate']}}

BlockSettle, a Swedish blockchain technology company, has today launched its flagship key management tool, the BlockSettle Terminal, and announced that it offers non-custodial trading and settlement in Bitcoin.

The BlockSettle Terminal is specifically designed to allow participants to remain in control of their Bitcoin balances while enjoying the benefits of an efficient and competitive market structure. The model is built on Bitcoin settlement taking place directly between participants whilst cash settlement takes place across BlockSettle accounts.

“We are pleased to offer a model built around the participants’ custodianship over their own blockchain assets, coupled with direct access to bilateral matching and settlement. Interested parties are encouraged to apply to become participants with our service.” said Albert Janson, COO, BlockSettle.

The BlockSettle Terminal is SegWit based and supports automated trading strategies, bespoke security models in relation to local, remote, and offline signing, and relies on the ArmoryDB and Satoshi client for network connectivity.

“Given that the efficiencies offered by blockchain technologies is built around stakeholder control of the ledger, it’s vital to have the tools to take advantage of this core premise. Our offering showcases the extent of BlockSettle’s service and brings a welcome benchmark which we hope the market will come to appreciate.” said Scott Millar, CEO, BlockSettle.

About BlockSettle

BlockSettle was established to develop matching and settlement models focused on blockchain ledger technologies.

BlockSettle offers a network of participants who are matched and settled bilaterally, in real-time, without incurring the risks associated with third-party custodianship over blockchain assets.
