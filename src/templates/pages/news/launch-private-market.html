BlockSettle announces the launch of its private market and opens trading in its own share

Malmo, Sweden, {{news['launchprivatemarket']['fullDate']}}

BlockSettle, a Swedish blockchain technology company, today announced the launch of its Private Market, a fully automated issuance and settlement service for Private Limited Companies where delivery and payment (DvP) is based on atomic Bitcoin swaps.

The company further announced that it will list its own share as the first actionable use-case and proof-of-concept for the service, through the investors’ holding company BlockSettle Partners AB (Ticker: BLK).

The Private Market offers a registry model where shares are held in the investor’s name on the legal register while coloured coins are issued as representations of the shares on the Bitcoin blockchain. The coloured coins are traded against Bitcoin in atomic coinjoin transactions directly between participants, where the legal register is automatically updated based on the settlement of transactions.

“BlockSettle is pleased with the efficiencies our Private Market brings to all stakeholders. Investors will have real-time access to liquidity while benefiting from a transparent and robust legal structure. Companies will enjoy automated administration of share registry updates and can focus on their core businesses”, says Albert Janson, COO, BlockSettle.

When both share representations and the consideration offered as payment reside on the same public ledger, it becomes possible to effect real-time settlement through the execution of an atomic swap utilising the BlockSettle Terminal.

“We are pleased to offer a model where Delivery versus Payment (DvP) happens at the point of execution directly between the participants to the trade and where we monitor transactions on the public blockchain and verify settlement finality.” said Scott Millar, CEO, BlockSettle.

ABOUT BLOCKSETTLE

BlockSettle was established to develop matching and settlement models focused on blockchain ledger technologies.

BlockSettle offers a network of participants who are matched and settled bilaterally, in real-time, without incurring the risks associated with third-party custodianship over blockchain assets.
