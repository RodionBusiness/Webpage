Registrar Service

BlockSettle offers a secure and cost-effective method of issuing representations of real-world assets on the blockchain in the form of tokens. It offers an alternative to the expensive and time-consuming task of processing instrument transfer requests by issuing blockchain tokens to the instrument owner as representations of their holdings. Token-holders can receive all the benefits of direct instrument ownership while being able to take advantage of real-time settlements.

Private Market

NEW ISSUES

For new issues, the process begins when a company or individual who wishes to issue existing instruments on the bitcoin blockchain contacts BlockSettle.

BlockSettle works with the sponsor and sets up a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) in Sweden. The SPV structure will allow for harmonized handling of the share registry so that the target investment can be based anywhere globally. The SPV outsources the maintenance of the share register to BlockSettle who issues representations in the form of tokens on the bitcoin blockchain. The Articles of Association of the SPV will be tailored to allow shares to be freely transferable and limits the scope of the business to only holding target company shares.
