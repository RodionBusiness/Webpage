Private Market

BlockSettle offers a Private Market for all tokenized assets over which we act as registrar. As a Market Participant of BlockSettle you are able to transact directly with other Participants where matching and delivery of tokenized assets versus bitcoin occur directly between Participants.

Registrar Service

DELIVERY VERSUS PAYMENT (DVP)

The BSAB System for settlement aims to guarantee Participants a final and irrevocable settlement through Delivery versus payment (DVP). This means, amongst other things, that no capital risk will arise for Participants during settlement, in other words, no Participant risks delivering to another Participant without receiving payment. Settlements are made on a gross basis in real-time by means of atomic coinjoin settlement.

AUTOMATIC DATA PROCESSING

BlockSettle creates a register for each SPV in the BlockSettle system. For account balances, BlockSettle issues tokens on the bitcoin blockchain and distributes them to the account holder. All account updates concerning the account will be executed by the Participant by means of assigning specific UTXOs to its trade counterpart in exchange for bitcoin in a coinjoin transaction. Once the transaction is mined six blocks deep, BlockSettle updates the share registry.

Why settle through BlockSettle

  • Access to a liquid secondary market
  • Global participant base
  • Real-time Delivery-versus-Payment
  • All activities are entirely based on automatic data processing
