Participants

Any participant wishing to trade directly at BlockSettle must be admitted as a BlockSettle Participant.

Spot XBT Dealing

Market Data Participant

Market Data Participants use our Terminal as a key management tool and have access to our market data.

Requirements
  • Create an account in the BlockSettle System
  • Party to the Market Data Agreement

Market Participant

Market Participants are direct participants who can submit Request-For-Quote (RFQ) in all products and formulate responsive quotes in Spot FX and Private Market products.

Requirements
  • Non-US tax subject
  • Not a Politically Exposed Person (PEP)
  • Successfully undergo a due diligence review
  • Have technical systems in place which conform with our matching and settlement model requirements
  • Make a minimum initial cash deposit of EUR 1,000 or equivalent in any of the currencies offered
  • Party to the Participant Agreement

Dealing Participant

Dealing Participants are participants in the system who, in addition to Market Participants, can formulate responsive quotes to Spot XBT trading interest.

Requirements
  • Minimum cash balance of EUR 5,000 or equivalent on account
  • Fill-to-Quote ratio of 85%
  • Party to the Dealing Participant Agreement
