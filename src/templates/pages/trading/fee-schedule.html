Fee Schedule

BlockSettle aims to maintain competitive fee levels. Fees for trading and settlement at BlockSettle will vary by product and product group and may include, but are not limited to, annual subscription fees, one-off application fees, connectivity fees, trading fees, settlement fees, and market data fees.

Application Fee

BlockSettle charges a non-refundable Application Fee prior to reviewing any application. The fee comprises the cost of issuing and sending a One-Time Password through the postal service and a EUR 10 account credit to enable an Authentication Address submission. Unsuccessful applications forfeit their fee.

Application Fee EUR 15

Blockchain Network Fee

Network transaction fees must be sufficiently high to ensure that a transaction is included in the next mined block. The transaction fee is processed by and received by the miner who includes the transaction in a block.
