Spot FX

Contracts are DDD/CCC, where DDD and CCC represent different currencies. The quantity of an order is the amount of DDD, the base currency. The price is CCC, the quote currency, per unit DDD. Only currencies deliverable by book-entry shifts in the BlockSettle system are allowed for this contract.
