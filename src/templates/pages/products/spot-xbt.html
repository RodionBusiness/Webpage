Spot XBT

Contracts are traded XBT/CCC, where XBT represents bitcoin and CCC a deliverable cash currency held on BlockSettle’s books. The quantity of an order is the amount of XBT or the amount of CCC. The price is CCC, the quote currency, per unit of XBT, the base currency.
