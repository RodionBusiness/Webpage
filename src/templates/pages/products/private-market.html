Private Market

Contracts are Tokenized Asset/XBT, where the Tokenized Asset represents the real-world asset issued on the bitcoin blockchain over which BlockSettle acts as the registrar, and XBT represents bitcoin. Tokenized assets are issued on the bitcoin blockchain in the form of coloured coins. The quantity of an order is the amount of Coloured Coins, the base currency. The price is XBT, the quote currency, per unit of the Tokenized Asset.
